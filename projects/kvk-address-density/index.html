Method

What the score measures

The model compares business density with building capacity and comparable areas. In a real application, its inputs would consist only of lawfully obtained establishment data and open area or building registers.

  1. Normalise establishments to a chosen aggregation level.
  2. Link BAG indicators such as usable floor area and occupancy units.
  3. Compare density and industry mix with comparable areas.
  4. Flag outliers as priorities for further investigation.
Data limitation: the open KVK dataset does not contain complete addresses or business identifiers for public republication. This demo therefore only simulates aggregated indicators.
