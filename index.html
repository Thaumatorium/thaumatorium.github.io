Why use SON

Because you can use it to share webpages, whether it's a news article, meme (text or image), video, music, or app, save them, export them, and best of all: find related things. Got a low-res version of a meme? Upload it to Imgur, post it on SON, fill in some data and see if a higher res version pops up! Read an article where you wonder if someone reacted to it? Find it on SON and check it out!
